The Jolly Miller Cafe, managed by its current owner since 1989

Since 1989, the current manager Brian Parle has owned the cafe, working to provide an active and lively cafe that customers will return to over and over again.


Free wifi!

As of last year, we have installed BT internet for the cafe, therefore we have free wifi for our customers, just ask for the code and connect!

What we offer

The cafe makes an excellent full english breakfast, while having a diverse stock of drinks and an ample seating area. The cafe is full of regulars who love a cooked breakfast with a cup of tea.

Clean environment

The menu page shows an image of the entire menu along with the dietary requirements of these foods. Friendly atmosphere

The cafe always has its locals in, as it provides a friendly environment that feels welcoming and lively. Full of both Everton and Liverpool fans, there is always a football discussion.
